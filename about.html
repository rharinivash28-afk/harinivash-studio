About

Hey, I'm Harinivash.

I build automation workflows, Notion systems, AI agents, and websites for businesses that are tired of tools that don't talk to each other.

Most people connect tools. I design the system underneath them — the one that handles edge cases, doesn't break on weekends, and doesn't need you to babysit it. If your team is still manually moving data between apps every week, that's not an ops problem. That's a missing system, and that's exactly what I build.

I'm not the loudest person in the room, but I'm probably the last one to give up on a problem. I don't take on everything — I work with a small number of clients at a time so I can actually focus. If we work together, you're not getting handed off to a junior. You get me, start to finish.

I work remotely with clients across India, the US, and Europe. I care about building things right, not just building things that look like they work in a demo.

What I value

  • Production-grade, not demos. Error handling, retries, edge cases — covered without you asking.
  • Plain language. No jargon walls. You'll always understand what you're getting.
  • Ownership. Full documentation and a walkthrough so you're never dependent on me.
  • Honesty. If automation isn't the right fit for your problem, I'll tell you.
