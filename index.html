Process

How I work.

No black box. No dropped handoffs. Every project follows the same six steps, from first call to final delivery.

01

Discovery

We get on a call. You walk me through your stack, your problem, and what a good outcome looks like. I ask the questions you haven't thought to answer yet. Nothing gets built until I fully understand what needs to be built.

02

Scoping

I come back with a clear breakdown: what gets built, how it works, what done looks like. You approve it. No surprises mid-build.

03

Build

This is where I go heads down. Production-grade workflows and systems, not prototypes. Error handling, edge cases, deduplication, retry logic — all covered without you having to ask.

04

Testing & QA

I break it before you do. Every workflow gets tested against real scenarios, failure cases, and edge inputs. You only see it when it's ready.

05

Handoff

A live walkthrough call, a video recording you can rewatch, and full documentation explaining how it works and how to maintain it. You leave knowing exactly what you have.

06

Ongoing Support

If you want someone on call for updates, fixes, and additions, I offer a monthly retainer after handoff. Completely optional. No pressure — but it's there if you want the work to keep evolving.
